Honey Dijon Mustard Pretzel Dip

Try this easy, delicious dip recipe to pair with your fresh-baked soft pretzels!

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons grained Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice
  • Pretzels, for dipping

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, whisk the mayonnaise, honey, mustard and lemon juice together until combined.
  2. Serve at room temperature or chilled with pretzels for dipping.
